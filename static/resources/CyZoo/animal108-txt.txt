It lives in the central and southern Europe, the Middle East, in Africa and in North American forests.

It eats herbs and different bugs.

It is long-eared and short-tailed, with long hind legs and, usually, gray or brown fur. Its young are born fully haired, with open eyes, and advanced enough to hop about a few minutes after birth.

In northern latitudes they are white in winter and usually grayish brown in summer. Elsewhere, they are usually gray brown throughout the year.
