It's found in the forest areas of west-central China and subsists mainly on bamboo.

It has a distinctive coloration, consisting of a dense creamy white coat of fur marked with a broad black band across the shoulders and forelegs, black hind legs, and blobs of black colour on the ears and around the eyes.

The average animal eats 15-30 kg (33-66 pounds) of bamboo leaves, stems, and shoots a day.

It looks very much like a bear.
