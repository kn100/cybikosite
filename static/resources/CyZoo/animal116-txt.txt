This predator's habitats range widely from British Columbia to Patagonia, in areas as varied as mountains, deserts, and jungles.

It hunts by night and preys on a variety of animals, including deer, wild hog, peacock, and there have been recorded instances of its attacking elephants and adult buffalo.

Its voice is like that of a domestic cat, only louder.

Its coloration is from pale buff to reddish brown. The ears and tail are generally dark, while the rump and belly are white. They have no mane.
