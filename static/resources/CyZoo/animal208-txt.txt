It can be found in northwestern Africa, across Eurasia, and in western North America.

A bird of farmlands and tree-studded open country, it eats insects, seeds, small vertebrates, the eggs and young of other birds, and fresh carrion.

It is black-and-white bird with a long, iridescent blue-green tail. It makes a large, round nest of twigs cemented with mud.

People usually say that someone who talks too much is blithering like this bird.
