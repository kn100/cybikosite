This creature, the largest flightless bird in existence, lives in Africa, the southern United States and Australia.

It lives mainly on plant matter, but does eat some animal food.

The male is mostly black but has white plumes in the wings and tail; females are mostly brown. The head and most of the neck, reddish to bluish in color, is lightly feathered; the legs, including the powerful thighs, are naked. The head is small, the bill short and rather wide; the big brown eyes have thick black lashes.

When frightened, this bird can achieve a speed of 65 km per hour (40 miles per hour). If cornered, it can also deliver dangerous kicks.
