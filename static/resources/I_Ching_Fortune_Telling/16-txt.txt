         Yu.                   Liberty            You may take liberties,  and act the way you wish,which will make you      responsible for your     decisions. The future    mainly depends on your   ability to make a correctdecision and choose the  right track. The time is favorable to all kinds ofentertainment, but don't forget your sense of     proportion. You'll be a  success and lucky in gameand in any new pursuits. Your wishes will be      fulfilled without fail,  but you must wait a      little while.
