          Xu.                   Waiting           Your intensions will     successfully be realized only if you are ready to wait. In a very short    time, you will need all  your strengths. Don't    hurry: hasty doings now  will only cause harm.    Moreover, after a while, you will meet someone whowill help you. Your      financial position will  soon be improved. If you plan ahead and are quite prudent, your wishes willcertainly be fulfilled.
